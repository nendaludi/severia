About convocation

OVERVIEW OF THE SEVENTH CONVOCATION OF THE EASTERN AFRICA STATISTICAL TRAINING CEMTRE

The seventh convocation of the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre of the year 2024 continues the tradition within academic institutions f gathering together academicians,students,alumni,benefactors and industry proffesionals as stakeholders of the university community,it is the time to exchange viewson topical issues that touch teaching,research, and professional development.

This year's convocation has selected the topic "THE ROLE OF OFFICIAL STATISTICS AND BIG DATA IN THE ECONOMIC TRANSFORMATION AND SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT OF TANZANIA" as the standing point to reflect on the teme states "Modernizing data ecosystems to accelerate the implementation of African Continental Free Trade Area (AFCTA): The role of official statistics and big data in the ecnomic transformation and sustainable development of Africa"

African statistics Day is celebrated each Year on 18th of November.The commemoraion aims among others, to increase public awareness about the imporntant role which statistics play in all aspects of social and economic development. This Year's convocation aims to understand the African Continental Free Trade Area (AFCTA) and the potential role statistics and big data plays in discovering markets and serving customers across Africa.

The President of the convocation day is the dean of students: Ms.Nyambilila Minga

The Guest of Honour: Prof.Fortunata Songora Makene

Various awards will be given to the students who out-performed in various courses and fields provided at the Eastern Africa Statistical Training Centre(EASTC), The students organisation (EASTCSO), awards from various department in the centre such as Head of Research Consultancy and Publications Department's Prize
